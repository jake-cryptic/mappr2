Terms of Uploading Read before Uploading

Before uploading images you must read the following information:

Uploading images means that you are granting Mappr a license to use, modify, share, distribute your images; this permission can be revoked at any time by deleting the image from Mappr.
Information associated with the image will also be uploaded, this metadata can be removed by external tools before uploading or by updating settings on the image details page after upload.
You agree to only upload the following:
  • photos of mobile equipment (i.e. antennas / radio units),
  • photos containing mobile equipment (i.e. picturesque landscape shot of mast on a hill),
  • screenshots of mobile applications relevant to the discussion of mobile equipment (i.e. NSG screenshots)
You must also have permission to use and share the images you upload (no Google Maps screenshots).
Preferably, photos should be high resolution and taken using a decent camera.
Failure to comply with these terms will result in immediate account suspension.

Information to consider before uploading:

  • You must make an effort to add alt-text to your photos, this text describes the contents of the image to users with sight impairments.
  • You should make an effort to add a description to your photos, this will add further context to the photos.
  • You should avoid uploading visually similar images.
If you are uploading images on a phone and want to preserve the location data from the image, please upload it via a PC
